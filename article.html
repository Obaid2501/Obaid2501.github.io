From the Desk of the Director
EXCERPT FROM AN INTERVIEW WITH DR. B. K. MISHRA, DIRECTOR, IIT GOA
Jan 18, 2018

Directors namely, Prof. Manoj Singh Gaur (IIT Jammu), Prof. P. Seshu (IIT Dharwad), Prof. K.N. Satyanarayana (IIT Tirupati), Prof. Rajat Moona (IIT Bhilai) and Prof. C.V.R. Murty (IIT Jodhpur) participated in this meeting to discuss various administrative and academic issues.

Prof. P.B. Sunil Kumar, Director, IIT Palakkad joined the meeting through Skype. Prof. B K Mishra, Director, IIT Goa presided over the meeting.
